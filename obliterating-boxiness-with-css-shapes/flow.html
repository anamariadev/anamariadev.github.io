The Dusty Promise Program is designed to cover tuition and mandatory fees by first utilizing federal, state, and institutional aid, like Federal Pell and TEXAS Grants, scholarships and other gift aid. The Dusty Promise Program will cover any remaining tuition and mandatory fees not covered by these programs. View our comprehensive Dusty Promise FAQs for more details.

Students interested in being considered must meet all eligibility requirements and deadlines. Upon meeting these criteria, students are automatically opted into the program; no additional application needs to be submitted.

First-Time Freshman: A student that has not begun their post-secondary studies at another college or university after graduating from high school or earning a GED.

New Transfer: A student that already started their post-secondary studies at another college or university after graduating from high school or earning a GED.
